Our Story

Cultivating
Excellence

Founded in Kanakapura, Karnataka, Sri Flora has been the trusted source of premium floriculture products for over 15 years. Nestled in the fertile lands of Bantanala, our farm spans acres of vibrant greenhouses and open fields — home to over 500 varieties of plants and flowers.

We serve nurseries, event decorators, and farmers across South India, maintaining uncompromising standards from seed to delivery. Our mission is simple: grow the most beautiful, healthy plants and deliver them to partners who share our passion for nature.
